MovieMator Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how MovieMator ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our movie tracking application (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our Service to you.

a. Information You Provide Directly to Us:

When you create an account, track movies, or interact with our Service, you directly provide us with information, including:

  • Account Information: Your email address, username, and password (hashed/encrypted) when you register for an account. This is managed through AWS Cognito.
  • Movie Tracking Data: Information about the movies you've watched, including titles, watch dates, ratings, reviews, notes, and any other details you choose to add.
  • User Preferences: Any settings or preferences you configure within the app.
  • Public Profile Setting: Your choice to make your profile public or keep it private.

b. Automatically Collected Information:

When you access and use the Service, certain information may be automatically collected from your device:

  • Authentication Tokens: We store secure authentication tokens (JSON Web Tokens - JWTs) in your browser's local storage. These tokens are essential for keeping you logged in and maintaining your authenticated session as you navigate the app. These are not traditional "cookies" but serve a similar functional purpose for session management.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated data about how you interact with our Service, such as pages visited, features used, and time spent. This helps us understand user behavior and improve the app. This data is generally anonymized and not linked directly to your personal identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To create and manage your account, authenticate your login, and enable all core functionalities of the app, such as adding, editing, and viewing your movie tracking data.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: To display your movie history, ratings, and provide personalized statistics and insights based on your tracked movies.
  • To Enable Public Profiles: If you choose to make your profile public, to display your selected profile information and movie tracking data to other users and the general public, allowing them to view and search your shared content.
  • To Improve Our Service: To understand how users interact with the app, identify areas for improvement, and develop new features.
  • To Communicate with You: To send you service-related notifications (e.g., password reset emails).
  • For Security: To protect the integrity and security of our Service and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized access.

3. How We Store and Share Your Information

a. Data Storage:

  • User Account Data: Your account credentials (email, hashed password) are securely managed by Amazon Web Services (AWS) Cognito, a robust authentication service.
  • Movie Tracking Data: Your movie watching history, ratings, reviews, and associated data are stored in a secure PostgreSQL database.
  • Client-Side Storage: Authentication tokens are stored in your browser's local storage to maintain your logged-in session. This data is only accessible by your browser and our application.

b. Data Sharing:

  • Public Profiles: If you choose to set your profile as public, your username, account creation and update dates, and all your movie tracking data (including titles, watch dates, ratings, and reviews) will be accessible to other users and the general public through the Service. By enabling this feature, you provide your explicit consent for this public sharing of your data. You can change your public profile setting at any time in the Edit Profile page.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We use AWS Cognito for user authentication and management. AWS operates under its own strict privacy policies. We do not share your personal movie tracking data with third-party service providers for their own marketing or advertising purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our Service of any such change in ownership or control of your personal information.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your data. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the information we collect from accidental, unlawful, or unauthorized destruction, loss, alteration, access, disclosure, or use. These measures include data encryption, secure server environments, and access controls.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions. If your profile is public, exercising this right will also remove your data from public visibility.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions. This includes the ability to change your public profile setting to private.
  • Right to Object to Processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at tudororban2@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.
